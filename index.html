Automatic Blackboard Writing and Cleaning

Anshad K, Shahal Thayyil, Jishna P, Rashmi V R

An innovative project designed to enhance the efficiency and functionality of traditional blackboards in educational environments. The system integrates advanced components such as stepper motors, servo motors, Arduino Uno, and A4988 motor drivers to automate the writing and cleaning process.

IJSR 2023

Automatic Diabetes Diagnosis & Drug Delivery Device

Anshad K, et al.

This study presents a portable, non-invasive device that monitors blood glucose levels and automatically delivers insulin based on variations. Using painless sensing technology, it calculates and injects the required dosage—offering a smart, needle-free solution for diabetes care.
